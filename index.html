Breaking the barrier to Tech

Making Tech Inclusive and Accesible

We are dedicated to breaking down the barriers that often hinder individuals from accessing opportunities in the tech industry. Understanding the challenges faced by aspiring tech enthusiasts, we actively work to eliminate obstacles and create a level playing field.

By providing accesible training programs, mentorship, and a supportive community, we ensure that no one is left behind die to financial constraints, lack of resources, or limited networks.

Through our inclusive approach, we are democratizing tech education and opening doors for individuals from diverse backgrounds to pursue their passion and thrive in the tech field.

Timeless Skills

Enabling Africans with Essential Skills for Today and Tomorrow

Zuri is on a mission to empower Africans by equipping them with essential skills requored both in the present and future. Recognising the transformative power of technology, we provide comprehensive training and resources to enable African youth to thrive in the digital age.

By offering cutting-edge tech skills, we ensure that individuals are prepared to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving technological landscape. Through this empowerment, we are paving the way for African youth to unlock their full potential and become valuable contributors to the global tech community.

Training Programs

Entry level and intermediate training and up-skill programs

Zuri Training

We understand a complete beginner cannot become a professional after a few weeks, the aim here is to get you familiar enough with each of the tools such that you'll be able to continue learning on your own with minimal support.

02

Zuri Internship

We place you in a work simulation. We give you tasks every week, the tasks ranges from easy to difficult as you progress from stage to stage, making it to the final stage means you are ready for actual work.
